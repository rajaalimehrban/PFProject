**Medium Programming Questions (1–50)**
01. What is the output of `print("3" + "4")` in Python?
A. 7
B. 34
C. Error
D. 3+4
**Answer: B**

02. Which of the following is a mutable data type in Python?
A. tuple
B. list
C. str
D. int
**Answer: B**

03. What will be the output of `len(set([1,2,2,3]))`?
A. 2
B. 3
C. 4
D. 1
**Answer: B**

D. super
